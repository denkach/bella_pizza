Since 1987

Three Generations of Passion

It began with a wood-fired oven, a hand-written recipe, and a belief that great pizza brings people together. Giuseppe Bella opened our doors in 1987 — today his grandchildren keep the fire burning.

Every dough is proofed for 48 hours. Every sauce is made from San Marzano tomatoes. Nothing has changed, because nothing needed to.
